## Business context

Whether due to the adversities caused by the crisis or the fierce competitiveness of the market, it is essential to analyze all aspects of your business. This action allows you to understand how to position yourself before your customers and more, it stimulates advantages over your competition. However, there is no point in having an absurd amount of data, because if it cannot be transformed into relevant information, it offers little to the business.

In 2021, Gartner found that most large companies are expected to have more market value for their amount of useful information than for the services and products offered. This is also confirmed when we take social media as an example: We see that its value is inherent in the quantity and quality of information that the company has about users. Returning a little to the data management solution, we can say that it helps and simplifies the management of your company's internal and external data. In this way, you increase business intelligence, become more efficient, and better understand your operational capacity. In addition, there is the possibility of crossing information, facilitating the recognition of trends, patterns, bottlenecks and opportunities.
