# 5 goals of a strong data management strategy Simplify the process of analyzing vital business information.

### 5 goals of a strong data management strategy
Simplify the process of analyzing vital business information.

Whether due to the adversities caused by the crisis or the fierce competitiveness of the market, it is essential to analyze all aspects of your business. This action allows you to understand how to position yourself before your customers and more, it stimulates advantages over your competition. However, there is no point in having an absurd amount of data, because if it cannot be transformed into relevant information, it offers little to the business.

In 2021, Gartner found that most large companies are expected to have more market value for their amount of useful information than for the services and products offered. This is also confirmed when we take social media as an example: We see that its value is inherent in the quantity and quality of information that the company has about users. Returning a little to the data management solution, we can say that it helps and simplifies the management of your company's internal and external data. In this way, you increase business intelligence, become more efficient, and better understand your operational capacity. In addition, there is the possibility of crossing information, facilitating the recognition of trends, patterns, bottlenecks and opportunities.

#### Improve customer relationship and retention
Data management has a number of advantages, but it is in business strategy where its impact is undeniable. This is because the analysis of the information allows the company:

- Define precisely the consumption profile of your potential customer
- Facilitate the creation of communication strategies
- Promote marketing actions

#### Be more assertive when interacting with the target audience
Have a greater perception of the characteristics of customers, thus influencing their satisfaction. Therefore, when your sales force has accurate, organized information and applies it as the basis for structured analysis, it becomes more efficient. After all, at the end of the day, all this allows us to respond with assertiveness to market demands and movements, with more assertive offers, products, services, approaches and communication. Even so, one of the potential results of the good use of information with data management is the increase in the customer retention rate, since the organization knows their purchase history and the evolution of their behavior, and thus can devise best strategies to stimulate engagement with the company.

#### Reduces operating costs
Who isn't looking for cost savings, right? This is an advantage that can impact the rest of the actions, because, without savings, how to inject resources into the appropriate strategies? When the company does not have a consolidated database, any analysis becomes complex and this directly implies financial resources, since poor decision making has a negative impact on financial health. On the other hand, the modern data management solution increases the relevance and assertiveness of the company's data analysis. From well-structured data storage, analyzes become more objective and accurate, based on data captured and organized in a recurring, uninterrupted and secure way. Therefore, we can say that the company analyzes better, in less time and supported in a real context.

#### Strengthens information security and alleviates administrative obstacles
Who has not yet made information security a priority, will surely face big problems in the future. And we are not only talking about LGPD compliance, no, but the negative impact that the loss and loss of data have on the brand in the market. To take into account, Brazil currently ranks only behind Mexico in the ranking of corporate data fraud. The data management solution is highly scalable and offers advanced security tools. In this way, your company has a high level access control, both in the applications and in the network.

In addition, there are the cryptographic layers that make up the technology in the cloud, preventing unauthorized access. As a result, you will have a significant reduction in internal and external threats. We refer to bottlenecks caused by user errors, cyberattacks, hacker invasions and many other threats that are part of the information universe. Therefore, the best way to mitigate problems and ensure information security is through advanced data management.

#### Streamline processes with higher data availability and quick responses
Decision-making has never been more important than it is today, but this requires agility in accessing information, in order to identify the best way forward, right? However, legacy systems no longer offer the agility that the market requires. A management system offers online data and quick responses. In fact, quick responses are the result of information organization and availability, enabling instant benchmarking and high-performance batch reporting. We can say that it is technology that opens possibilities.

Keeping these 5 goals in mind when you creating a data management strategy will help you can significantly reduce the problems in managing large volumes of data and leverage data as a strategic asset for the company that drives operational value
